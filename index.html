Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming the way we live, work, and communicate. It has become an essential part of modern digital life.

  • AI powers social media algorithms, smart assistants, and recommendation systems.
  • It influences how information is shared and consumed online.
  • This project aims to spread awareness about how AI works and its impact on society.
  • It promotes responsible and ethical use of AI technologies.
  • Understanding AI helps individuals make informed and safe digital decisions.

Project Objectives

The primary objectives of the Everyday AI project are:

  • To create awareness about Artificial Intelligence in everyday life.
  • To explain basic AI concepts in simple and understandable language.
  • To promote ethical and responsible use of AI technologies.
  • To enhance digital literacy among students and community members.

Scope of Awareness

The scope of the Everyday AI project focuses on spreading awareness about how Artificial Intelligence is used in daily applications such as virtual assistants, recommendation systems, smart devices, and generative AI tools.

The initiative primarily targets students and young adults, while also including educators and the general public. It emphasizes understanding AI concepts, ethical concerns, privacy issues, and responsible digital behavior.
